Ocean Freight Services by Xiamine Cargo

Less than Container Load (LCL)

  • Share a container with other shipments.
  • Pay only for the space you use.
  • Requires proper packaging and labeling.

Full Container Load (FCL)

  • Entire container used by one shipper.
  • Faster and more secure than shared shipments.
  • Cost-effective for large or bulk cargo.

Sustainable deliveries & collections

  • Eco-friendly vehicles for low emissions.
  • Minimal and recyclable packaging.
  • Consolidated shipment to reduce trips.

FAQs

Questions?
Glad you asked

1

Do you handle international shipments?

Yes, we offer international shipping to a wide range of countries. Please check our shipping policy or contact customer support for specific destination availability.

2

What types of shipping services do you offer?

We provide several shipping options including standard, expedited, and express services. The available options may vary depending on your location and the items ordered.

3

Can I track my shipment?

Absolutely. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to monitor your shipment's progress in real time.

4

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on the shipping method and destination. Typically, international orders arrive within 5 to 15 business days. Express options may offer faster delivery.
