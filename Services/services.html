Our ocean freight services

Less than Container Load (LCL)

  • Share a container with other shipments.
  • Pay only for the space you use.
  • Requires proper packaging and labeling.

Full Container Load (FCL)

  • Entire container used by one shipper.
  • Faster and more secure than shared shipments.
  • Cost-effective for large or bulk cargo.
